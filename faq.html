Frequently Asked Questions



1.What is digital marketing?

Digital marketing is the promotion of products or services through online platforms such as search engines, websites, and social media.

2.Why is SEO important?

SEO improves a website’s visibility on search engines which helps attract more visitors and potential customers.

3.How long does digital marketing take to show results?

Some strategies like PPC ads show quick results while SEO and content marketing may take several months.

4. How much do your services cost?

The cost depends on the type of service and the size of the project. We offer different packages to fit different budgets.

5. Do you create content for social media?

Yes, we design posts, write captions, and manage social media accounts to help brands stay active online.

6. Can you redesign my existing website?

Yes, we can improve the design, speed, and performance of your current website.

7. How do you measure marketing success?

We track results using tools that measure website traffic, leads, engagement, and conversions.

8. Will my website work on mobile phones?

Yes, we create responsive websites that work smoothly on mobiles, tablets, and desktops.

9. Do you provide reports for campaigns?

Yes, we provide regular reports showing the performance and progress of marketing campaigns.

10. Why should I choose your agency?

We focus on creative ideas, data-driven strategies, and helping businesses grow online.
